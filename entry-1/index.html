THE JACK THIEF

Players: Three - ten
Things needed - a deck of cards
It could be played anywhere- in a bus, car or even in a flight
with a group of people sitting close to each other.

OBJECTIVE:

Get rid of the cards

INSTRUCTIONS

Discard a Jack from the deck
Distribute the cards to all the players equally and getting one less or more card is fine.
Look at the cards and discard all the pairs with the same numbers.
When every body has discarded all the pairs they had, the game shall start.
The player that has the least number of cards in his hand shall pick a random card from the player on his right.
And if the picked card makes a pair with any of the other card in his hand, he shall discard the pair.
Similarly the next person on right will pick a card from the next player on his right an so on.
As soon as all of your cards got their pairs and you have no cards left in your hands, you shall win.
And the rest of the players will continue the same thing until all of their cars are also not discarded.
The last player will be left with a single Jack in his hand and will become the JACK THIEF.
