STICKS

Players: One - Any number
Things needed - the game set/ a bunch of chopsticks
It could be played anywhere with a stagnent floor

OBJECTIVE:

Collect as much sticks as possible

INSTRUCTIONS

1.


Take the bunch of sticks in your hand.

2.


Place the bunch on its tips on the ground in the centre of the circle in your hand.

3.


Leave the bunch and let the sticks fall.

4.


Now start picking up the sticks one by one without moving other sticks.

5.


Once the stick has been removed, it can be used by you as a help for the rest of the game.

6.


Keep on collecting untill you move any other stick.

7.


After the stick has been moved, the bunch shall be passed with the remaining sticks.

8.


Keep on playing until the bunch is completed. And the person with the most stics win.
